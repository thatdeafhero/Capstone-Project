What are the Symptoms?

You may not see any visual changes in early stages of Diabetic Retinopathy, but as the eye disease progresses, you may notice the blurry vision, in which will be gradual or sudden. There is no pain in the eye disease; however, if it is severe, it will lead to very high eye pressures.

The general symptoms are:

  • worsening vision / blurry vision
  • sudden vision loss
  • swelling
  • flashes
  • floaters
  • eye pain
  • fatigue
  • thirst
  • hunger
  • frequent urination
