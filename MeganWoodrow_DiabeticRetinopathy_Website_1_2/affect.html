HOW DOES IT AFFECT YOU?

Diabetic Retinopathy affects your vision. Your vision will become more blurry than what you are used to see. You may see some floaters in your vision, blocking some areas of your vision.

WILL I SEE BETTER?

However, in many cases, vision loss is permanent. Please understand that the treatment is not to restore the lost vision, it is to prevent or slow further vision loss. However, there are some treatments that may be able to worsen some certain parts of vision in an attempt to preserve central vision. Therefore, prevention remains the key to control Diabetic Retinopathy.
